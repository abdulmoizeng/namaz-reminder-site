Namaz (Salah) Reminder

Google Calendar Add-on · Automatically blocks prayer times

No servers • Data stays in your Google account

What it does

  • Creates a secondary calendar named “Prayer Times”.
  • Adds/updates daily Salah events (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha).
  • Uses your provided location to calculate times.

What it doesn’t do

  • No external data storage or tracking.
  • No access to your primary calendar events.

Permissions

  • Google Calendar: manage events only in the “Prayer Times” calendar.
  • User Properties: store your settings (location, preferences) in your Google account.

Revoke / Uninstall

  1. Google Calendar → Settings → Add-ons → Manage → Uninstall.
  2. Delete the “Prayer Times” calendar from My calendars.
