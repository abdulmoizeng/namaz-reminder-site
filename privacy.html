Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 27, 2025

TalibDeen (“we”, “our”, or “us”) provides Islamic digital tools such as the Namaz Reminder add-on, Zakat Calculator, and other services. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it in compliance with Google’s policies and applicable law.

Information We Access

  • Google Calendar: Our Namaz Reminder add-on may request access to your Google Calendar in order to create prayer time reminders.
  • User-provided data: If you enter information (e.g., location for accurate prayer times), it is used only to provide reminders and calculations.

How We Use Information

The information we access is used solely to:

  • Display prayer times and reminders in your calendar.
  • Provide Islamic tools such as fasting reminders, zakat calculations, and Hajj/Umrah guides.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party.
  • We do not use your data for advertising purposes.
  • We do not store your personal data on external servers — all data stays within your Google account unless explicitly provided by you.

Data Storage

All information is stored securely within your Google account (for example, calendar events). We do not maintain external copies of your personal data.

User Control

  • You can revoke permissions at any time from your Google Account permissions page.
  • You may uninstall our add-ons at any time from the Google Workspace Marketplace.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Effective Date”.
